A Transformative National Initiative

In response to escalating security threats, including the misuse of fire extinguishers as IEDs, illicit trafficking, and substandard manufacturing practices, the Nigerian government launched NFEC under the National Fire Data Repository Center (NFDRC).

Mission

To eradicate substandard fire extinguisher practices, enhance national security, and ensure compliance with the highest safety standards through regulation, innovation, and public awareness.

Vision

To position Nigeria as a global leader in fire safety by domesticating manufacturing, fostering collaboration, and deploying cutting-edge technology.

National Security

Prevent terrorist exploitation of extinguishers.

Public Safety

Reliable emergency response with certified equipment.

Economic Growth

Boost local jobs via Importation Quota Program (IQP).

Transparency

QR codes and validation portals for citizen verification.

Accountability

Eliminate illegal vendors through Vendor Aggregator Platform (VAP).
